macOS Installation

On macOS, your next step once you've downloaded the Gurobi Remote Services package from our website (e.g., gurobi_server11.0.1_macos_universal2.pkg for Gurobi 11.0.1) is to double-click on the installer and follow the prompts. By default, the installer will place the Gurobi Remote Services 11.0.1 files in /Library/gurobi_server1101/macos_universal2 (note that this is the system /Library directory, not your personal  /Library directory). Your <installdir> (which we'll refer to throughout this document) will be
/Library/gurobi_server1101/macos_universal2.
